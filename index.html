Cosmic Facts

  • Neutron stars are so dense, a sugar cube of their material weighs more than all of Mount Everest.
  • Time dilation near black holes can slow time drastically compared to Earth.
  • Dark energy makes up about 68% of the universe, yet remains a complete mystery.
  • Voyager 1 is over 20 billion km away and still sending data to Earth.
  • The CMB is the afterglow of the Big Bang—visible in every direction.
  • The Great Attractor is pulling galaxies, including ours, toward it at unimaginable speed.
