What is Voyager 1?

Voyager 1 is a space probe launched by NASA on September 5, 1977, to explore the outer planets and eventually interstellar space. It is the farthest human-made object from Earth, traveling at over 61,000 km/h.

Voyager 1 has provided groundbreaking data about the gas giants and is now in the region of space beyond our solar system known as interstellar space.
