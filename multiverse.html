Multiverse Theory: Infinite Realities

The Multiverse Theory proposes that our universe is just one of many — possibly infinite — universes that exist in parallel dimensions or separate realms of reality.

  • Quantum Mechanics: Some interpretations suggest every quantum decision spawns a new universe.
  • Cosmic Inflation: Early universe inflation might have created bubble universes with different laws of physics.
  • String Theory: Predicts multiple dimensions, possibly hosting other universes.
  • Popular Culture: Seen in Marvel, Rick and Morty, and other multiverse-inspired stories.

Though unproven, the idea expands our imagination and poses one of the biggest questions: Are we truly alone in reality, or just one version of countless others?
