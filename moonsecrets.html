The Moon: Friend or Mystery?

The Moon has always captivated humanity, from ancient mythology to modern science. But beneath its peaceful glow lies a series of enigmas and unanswered questions that challenge our understanding of Earth's only natural satellite.

Strange Structures & Shadows

NASA's own photographs have shown bizarre anomalies on the lunar surface — shadows that don't match terrain, dome-like objects, and what appear to be artificial constructs. While skeptics cry "lighting tricks," others suspect ancient ruins or hidden installations.

NASA Recordings & Silenced Missions

Some Apollo astronauts reportedly heard strange sounds while on the far side of the Moon. Rumors swirl of secret transmissions, lost footage, and audio tapes describing unexplained objects watching the missions from the surface or orbit.

Hollow Moon Theory

One of the most radical theories proposes that the Moon is hollow or at least not entirely natural. When a lunar module crashed onto its surface, it "rang like a bell," puzzling scientists. Could the Moon be an artificial satellite placed here by unknown hands?

Alien Bases: Myth or Reality?

Many UFO researchers believe that the Moon might house extraterrestrial bases on its dark side. Some even suggest that’s why no country has returned in decades. Is it off-limits? Are we being watched from the shadows of our own sky?

Why Are We Really Avoiding the Moon?

Despite incredible technological advances, humans haven’t returned to the Moon in over 50 years. Budget is often blamed, but some believe we’ve been warned away — by forces unknown. Are we truly alone up there, or are secrets too dangerous to expose?
